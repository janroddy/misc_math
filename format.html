The Game of Chaos

The rules:

  1. Choose a random starting point within the triangle ABC
  2. Roll die
  3. Connect the point
    • Connect the point with edge A, when you roll a 1 or 2
    • Connect the point with edge B, when you roll a 3 or 4
    • Connect the point with edge C, when you roll a 5 or 6
  4. Repeat step 2

How will the points be distributed in the triangle? As rolling a die is random, the points must be scattered "chaotically".
